Dual Axis Solar Based Smart Street And Weather Reporting System

The dual-axis solar-based smart street light and weather reporting system is an innovative project that combines energy-efficient lighting with weather monitoring.

It features solar panels equipped with dual-axis tracking to maximize energy generation by following the sun's movement.

Smart street lights integrated with motion and light sensors adjust their brightness for energy conservation and safety.

The system includes weather sensors for collecting temperature, humidity, air pressure, and rainfall data.

A centralized dashboard provides real-time insights into energy generation, street light status, and weather conditions.

Remote access enables monitoring and control, while energy optimization algorithms manage solar power usage.

The project aims to enhance sustainability, improve urban lighting, and provide valuable weather information for better decision-making.
